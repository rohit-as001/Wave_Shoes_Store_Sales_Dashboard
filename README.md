# Wave_Shoes_Store_Sales_Dashboard
### This Repository has Wave Shoes Store Sales projects.



# Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of a footwear retail business, focusing on sales performance, cost, transactions, and product distribution.
The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to explore data across States, Cities, Customers, Categories, and Order Types, helping uncover key business insights.

The goal of this project is to support data-driven decision-making in areas such as inventory planning, regional strategy, and product optimization.



## Business Objectives
- Monitor overall sales, cost, and profitability
- Analyze category and product-wise performance
- Identify top-performing states and cities
- Understand monthly sales trends
- Evaluate transaction volume and customer behavior
- Support strategic decisions for growth and efficiency



## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: ₹113.33M
- Total Cost: ₹71.23M
- Total Quantity Sold: 24.05K
- Total Transactions: 7.83K
- Product Categories: Casual, Formal, Semi-Casual
- Products: Brogues, Derby, Loafers, Oxford, Single Monk, Sneakers



# Key Insights

1) Category Performance

- Casual category (~₹55.45M) contributes the highest sales.
- Followed by Formal (~₹46.87M).
- Semi-Casual (~₹11M) has significantly lower demand.


2) Product Insights

  - Single Monk (35%) and Sneakers (~17%) dominate product sales.
  - Products like Brogues and Oxford Shoes contribute less, indicating niche demand.

  
3) Regional Performance

  - Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal are the top-performing states in terms of sales and profit.
  - Other states show moderate performance, suggesting opportunities for regional expansion.


4) Monthly Sales Trend

  - Peak sales observed in March (~₹20.87M).
  - Significant dip during June and July, indicating seasonal slowdown.
  - Recovery seen again in September and November.

    
5️) Transaction Analysis

  - High transaction volume in top states indicates strong demand concentration.
  - Balanced distribution across regions suggests stable market presence.


6) Top Performing States

  - Uttar Pradesh leads all states with ₹14.16M in sales and 1,002 transactions, followed closely by West Bengal (₹13.87M) and   
    Maharashtra (₹10.17M). These three states together account for a significant share of overall revenue, making them the core 
    geographic markets.


7) Profitability

  - With a total cost of ₹71.23M against revenue of ₹113.33M, the overall gross profit is approximately ₹42.10M (~37% margin).
    Uttar Pradesh generates the highest absolute profit of ₹5.21M, while even the 5th-ranked state — Madhya Pradesh — maintains
    a healthy ₹2.73M profit.    
    


## Business Recommendations

1. Focus on High-Demand Categories
  - Increase inventory and marketing for Casual and Formal footwear.
  - Reposition or redesign Semi-Casual products to boost demand.

2. Optimize Product Strategy
  - Promote top-selling products (Single Monk, Sneakers) through bundles and offers.
  - Introduce innovation or discounts for low-performing products.

3. Regional Expansion Strategy
  - Strengthen operations in top-performing states (UP, West Bengal).
  - Invest in marketing campaigns in underperforming regions to increase reach.

4. Seasonal Sales Planning
  - Prepare inventory and campaigns for peak months (March, September, November).
  - Run discounts and promotions during low-demand months (June–July).
    
5. Cost Optimization
  - Monitor cost trends to improve profit margins.
  - Optimize supply chain and vendor pricing.


6. Expand Reach in Mid-Tier States
  - States like Bihar, Gujarat, and Rajasthan show moderate numbers — they are not underperforming, but they are not being fully             exploited either. Targeted regional campaigns, regional influencer tie-ups, or city-level discounts could unlock meaningful growth 
    in these markets.

7. Grow the Online Channels
  - With a large number of on-shop transactions, there is clear headroom for online growth. Investing in digital marketing, a stronger       e-commerce presence, and delivery logistics in top-performing states could push transactions significantly higher.
